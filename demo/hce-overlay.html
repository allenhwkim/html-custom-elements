hce-overlay

hce-overlay opens layer on the top of existing element relatively positioned(default top)

Attributes

  • position: Default "top". Indicates where to show the overlay.
  • visible-by: Css selector to show the overlay. When the given element is focused or clicked, the overlay is displayed.
